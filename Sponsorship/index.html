Technical Team

Consists of some of the brightest and most engaged students at the University of Waterloo, complemented with unique training from UWAFT. Being a bronze or higher sponsor provides the opportunity to present your organization to the technical team and network with them.

UWAFT's engagement

In the local community helps build the brand image of sponsors' organizations. Additionally, the team helps to get kids engaged in school by visiting their schools and showing them the possibilities that come with attending and being involved with the university.
